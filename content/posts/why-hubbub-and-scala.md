### Why Scala?

Building **Hubbub** in Scala 3 has been an absolute joy. There's something uniquely powerful about a language that combines the best of object-oriented and functional programming while providing a type system that actually helps you sleep at night. 

Scala's expressiveness allowed us to build a robust, local-first facts service with minimal boilerplate. The new Scala 3 syntax is cleaner than ever, making it easier to focus on what matters: the logic and the user experience.

### A Debt of Gratitude: Thank You, Li Haoyi

We also want to take a moment to give a huge thanks to **Li Haoyi**. His work on the Scala ecosystem—from *Cask* and *Requests-Scala* to *uPickle* and *OS-Lib*—has fundamentally changed how we approach Scala development. 

Li Haoyi's philosophy of "simple, predictable, and robust" libraries is baked into the very DNA of Hubbub. By leveraging his tools, we were able to move fast without breaking things, creating a service that is both powerful and incredibly easy to maintain. If you haven't explored his work yet, you're missing out on some of the best engineering in the JVM world.

### Hubbub: Your Facts, Your Way

So, why Hubbub? In an era where AI agents are everywhere, the biggest challenge isn't the AI—it's the **context**. 

Hubbub is our answer to that challenge. It's a local-first, SQLite-backed service that lets you capture insights from Gemini and ChatGPT with a single shortcut. No cloud, no tracking, just your facts.

But Hubbub is more than just a storage layer. It's designed for **console agents**. By providing a grounded, structured context of your project's facts, Hubbub allows your agents to create and refine projects with a level of precision that was previously impossible.

### Try It Out

Hubbub is local-first and built for developers who care about their data. Whether you're building the next big thing or just organizing your thoughts, Hubbub is here to help you turn "noise" into "knowledge."
